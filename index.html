How I work

Clear steps from idea to production.

  1. Frame

    Translate business needs into KPIs, constraints, and datasets. Define reproducible scope.

  2. Model

    Formulate in Pyomo/PyPSA with tests, scenarios, and CI. Open, well-documented code.

  3. Validate

    Back-test vs. historicals; sensitivity & stress testing; performance profiling.

  4. Deploy

    Deliver notebooks, dashboards, or lightweight APIs. Handover + training.
